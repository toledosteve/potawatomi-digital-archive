# About This Collection

This collection preserves audio and video recordings of Potawatomi elders and community members sharing their stories, memories, and knowledge.

## What You'll Find

- **Elder Interviews** - Recorded conversations with elders sharing family histories and life experiences
- **Language Recordings** - Traditional stories and phrases in the Potawatomi language
- **Event Recordings** - Documentation of pow wows, ceremonies, and community gatherings
- **Family Stories** - Personal narratives passed down through generations

## Access Information

Some recordings may have restricted access based on the wishes of the speakers or their families. Contact the archive for more information about access to specific recordings.

## Contributing

Have recordings to share? Visit the [main archive](/hub/contribute/) to submit audio or video recordings. We accept:
- Interviews with elders and community members
- Recordings of traditional stories or songs
- Documentation of community events
