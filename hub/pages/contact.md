### Contributing Materials

Ready to share your photographs, documents, or stories? Visit our [Contribute](/contribute/) page to learn about the submission process, or use our [online submission form](/submit/).

### Research Requests

If you're a researcher interested in accessing materials or learning more about our collections, please include the following in your inquiry:
- Your name and institutional affiliation (if any)
- A brief description of your research project
- The specific materials or topics you're interested in

### Technical Issues

Experiencing problems with the website? Please let us know:
- What page you were on
- What you were trying to do
- Any error messages you received
- Your browser and device type

## Collaboration Opportunities

We welcome partnerships with:

- **Tribal organizations** and cultural preservation programs
- **Libraries, archives, and museums** with related holdings
- **Educational institutions** interested in incorporating our materials into curricula
- **Researchers and historians** studying Potawatomi history and culture
- **Technology partners** interested in supporting digital preservation

If you're interested in collaborating, please reach out with a brief description of your organization and proposed partnership.

---

## Volunteer

Interested in volunteering with the archive? We're always looking for help with:

- Digitization and scanning
- Metadata entry and cataloging
- Transcription of documents and recordings
- Translation (Potawatomi and English)
- Community outreach

Contact us to learn about current volunteer opportunities.
