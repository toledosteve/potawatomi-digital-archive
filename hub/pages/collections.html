How Our Collections Work

Each item in our archive includes metadata that connects it to:

  • Families - Family names connected to the item (e.g., Toledo, Wabaunsee, Bourassa)
  • Subjects - Topics and themes (e.g., Trail of Death, Pow Wow, Treaties)
  • People - Individual names when known

This allows a single photograph to appear when browsing multiple families, helping you discover unexpected connections.
