# About This Collection

This collection preserves written stories, memoirs, and family histories contributed by Potawatomi community members.

## What You'll Find

- **Family Histories** - Genealogical narratives and family trees
- **Personal Memoirs** - First-person accounts of life experiences
- **Traditional Stories** - Written versions of stories passed down through generations
- **Research Narratives** - Historical research about ancestors and events

## Contributing

We welcome your stories. Visit the [main archive](/hub/contribute/) to submit:
- Written family histories or memoirs
- Genealogical research about Potawatomi families
- Personal narratives about cultural experiences
- Traditional stories (with appropriate permissions)
