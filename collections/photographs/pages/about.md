# About This Collection

This collection preserves photographs documenting the lives, families, and communities of the Potawatomi people from the 19th century to present day.

## What You'll Find

- **Family Portraits** - Formal and informal photographs of Potawatomi families
- **Community Gatherings** - Pow wows, reunions, ceremonies, and celebrations
- **Historical Photos** - Mission schools, early settlements, and historical events
- **Contemporary Images** - Modern community life and cultural activities

## Cross-Referencing

Each photograph is tagged with:
- **Families** - Family names appearing in or connected to the photograph
- **Subjects** - Topics and themes (e.g., "Pow Wow", "Family Portraits")
- **People** - Individual names when known

This allows you to discover connections across families and find all materials related to your ancestors.

## Contributing

Have photographs to share? Visit the [main archive](/hub/contribute/) to submit your family photographs. We accept:
- Original photographs or high-quality scans
- Any time period from 1800s to present
- Both formal portraits and candid shots
