## Share Your Heritage

Every photograph tells a story. Every document preserves a moment in time. Every recording captures a voice that might otherwise be lost. Your contribution—no matter how small—helps build our collective memory.

## What We're Looking For

We welcome a wide variety of materials related to Potawatomi history, culture, and family stories:

### Photographs
- Historical photographs of family members, communities, and events
- Images of traditional activities, ceremonies, and gatherings
- Photographs of significant places and landmarks
- Contemporary documentation of cultural practices

### Documents
- Letters, diaries, and personal correspondence
- Official documents (birth certificates, tribal records, military records)
- News clippings and publications
- Maps and land records
- Recipes and handwritten notes

### Audio & Video
- Oral history recordings
- Interviews with elders and community members
- Recordings of songs, stories, and language
- Home videos of family gatherings and events

### Stories & Memories
- Written family histories and genealogies
- Personal memories and recollections
- Traditional stories and teachings (with appropriate permissions)

### What Happens After You Submit

1. **Review**: Our team reviews all submissions for quality and appropriateness
2. **Processing**: Accepted materials are digitized (if needed) and cataloged
3. **Approval**: You'll receive notification when your materials are added to the archive
4. **Publication**: Materials appear in the appropriate collection(s)

---

## Submission Guidelines

### Image Requirements
- **Format**: JPEG, PNG, or TIFF
- **Resolution**: At least 300 DPI for photographs
- **Size**: Up to 50MB per file

### Audio/Video Requirements
- **Formats**: MP3, WAV, MP4, or MOV
- **Size**: Up to 500MB per file
- **Larger files**: Contact us for alternative upload options

### Metadata
Please provide as much information as possible about each item:
- **Title or description**
- **Date** (exact or approximate)
- **People pictured or involved**
- **Location** (where the item was created or the event took place)
- **Your relationship** to the material
- **Any restrictions** on how the material may be used

---

## Permissions & Rights

### What We Need From You

By submitting materials, you confirm that:
- You have the right to share these materials
- You grant the archive permission to display them publicly (unless restricted)
- You have obtained consent from any living individuals pictured or recorded

### Your Rights

- You retain ownership of your original materials
- You may request removal of materials at any time
- You may specify restrictions on how materials are used

### Sensitive Materials

Some materials may be culturally sensitive or contain private information. Please let us know if any items require special handling or restricted access.
