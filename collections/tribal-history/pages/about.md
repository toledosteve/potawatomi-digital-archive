# About This Collection

This collection documents the history, treaties, migration, and notable figures of the Potawatomi people.

## What You'll Find

- **Historical Documents** - Treaties, land cessions, and government records
- **Maps** - Traditional territories, treaty boundaries, migration routes
- **Photographs and Artwork** - Leaders, communities, and traditional life

## Historical Context

The Potawatomi are an Algonquian-speaking people originally from the Great Lakes region. Their name means "People of the Place of Fire."
