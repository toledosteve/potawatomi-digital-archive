# About This Collection

This collection preserves historical documents relating to the Potawatomi people, including treaties, correspondence, government records, and personal papers.

## What You'll Find

- **Treaties** - Official treaties between Potawatomi bands and the U.S. government
- **Government Records** - Allotment rolls, census records, and official correspondence
- **Personal Papers** - Letters, diaries, and family documents
- **Legal Documents** - Citizenship papers, land records, and court documents

## Historical Significance

These documents tell the legal and political story of the Potawatomi people, from early treaty negotiations through removal, allotment, and modern tribal recognition.

## Contributing

Have documents to share? Visit the [main archive](/hub/contribute/) to submit historical papers, letters, or official records.
