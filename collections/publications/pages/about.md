# About This Collection

This collection preserves published materials about the Potawatomi people, including books, newspaper articles, academic papers, and tribal newsletters.

## What You'll Find

- **Books** - Historical accounts and contemporary publications
- **Newspaper Articles** - Coverage of Potawatomi events and communities
- **Academic Papers** - Research on language, culture, and history
- **Newsletters** - Tribal and community publications

## Contributing

Have publications to share? Visit the [main archive](/hub/contribute/) to submit books, articles, or newsletters.
